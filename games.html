Stride Jump

Stride Jump is a simple jumper-platforming game. You have a very short amount of time before the platform you're standing on disappears, so you need to act fast! Upon landing on a platform, you're awarded 5 points, but you lose a point each time you jump. You can jump two times before ultimatly falling. This value can be restored to two by either landing on a platform or tapping a side wall.
